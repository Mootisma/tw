Notable Content

The stuff in my mod worth knowing about

Weapons

Melee
  • Hu Tao

    Right click to use Elemental Skill: Guide to Afterlife.
    Left click to charge forward with a 'Charged Attack.'

  • Thorns

    Left click to attack. Gain 1 SP when hitting an enemy.
    Right click when having 15 SP to activate Destreza.
    Passively gain boosted health regen outside of combat when holding this weapon.

  • Aventurine

    Left click to attack. Damage is based on the user's DEF.
    Right click to grant allies a shield based on the user's DEF.
    Attacks that do less damage than the shield's HP will be counted as a dodge, granting i-frames.

  • Beehunter and Jackie

    Left click to rapidly punch at a close range.
    Passively gain Physical Dodge when holding these weapons.

  • La Pluma

    Left click to slash towards the cursor. Gain 1 SP per second.
    Right click with 40 SP to activate Reap, increasing ATK and ASPD for 25 seconds.
    Passively disables life regen. Attacks recover 5 hp per enemy hit.

Ranged
  • Fiammetta

    Left click to shoot towards the cursor. Gain 1 SP when hitting an enemy.
    Right click with 15 SP to activate 'Reponite'.
    'Reponite' changes the attack to a vertical barrage aimed at the cursor.
    Passively lose health when holding this weapon in exchange for power.

  • Kroos

    Left click to shoot towards the cursor. Gain 1 SP when hitting an enemy.
    Attack with 4 SP to greatly enhance the attack.

  • Caper

    Left click to throw a boomerang towards the cursor. Gain 1 SP when hitting an enemy.
    Right click with 25 SP to activate Double the Fun.
    Double the Fun increases ATK and throws an additional boomerang.
    Can only attack while holding a boomerang.

Magic
  • Ningguang

    Left click to fire two gems at the cursor and gain 1 Star Jade.
    Right click to launch one gem. This attack is boosted by up to 3 Star Jades.

  • Qingque

    Left click to throw a jade tile.
    Right click to draw up to two jade tiles that are a different suit from the majority.
    Qingque can hold 4 jade tiles. When holding 4 jade tiles of the same suit, enhance the next attack.

  • Kafka

    Left click to attack at the cursor. If enemies hit are suffering from DoT,
    all DoTs currently placed on that enemy immediately produce DMG equal to 75% of their original DMG.
    Right click to fire Kafka's gun towards the cursor, inflicting a 'Shock' DoT.

  • Kafka

    Put nearby non-boss enemies to Sleep for 5 seconds.
    15s redeployment time.

  • Absinthe

    Left click to attack towards the cursor. Gain 1 SP per second.
    Right click to activate Ultimatum, changing Absinthe's attacks:
    Each attack turns into bursts of 4 attacks that each deal 90% damage, but
    enemies above 50% HP will only take 1 damage.

Summon
  • Herta

    Left click to summon a Herta to fight for you. Max 1 per person.
    Herta will fly towards enemies and periodically give them a big diamond.

  • Furina

    Left click to summon Furina's Salon Solitaire to fight for you. Max 1 per person.
    Furina's Salon Solitaire have independent attacks, and will drain the owner's HP in exchange for power.

  • Yelan

    Left click to summon Yelan's Depth-Clarion Dice to fight for you. Max 1 per person.
    Yelan's Depth-Clarion Dice will fire coordinated attacks aimed at the owner's cursor.

  • Xiangling

    Left click to summon Pyronado to fight for you. Max 1 per person.
    Pyronado will move around the owner's position in a circle.

  • Skadi the Corrupting Heart

    Left click to summon Skadi the Corrupting Heart. Max 1 per person.
    Skadi the Corrupting Heart does not attack, instead increasing nearby allies' health regen.
    After 56 seconds, Skadi the Corrupting Heart activates her skill, buffing nearby allies' ATK and DEF.

  • Mostima

    Left click to summon Mostima. Max 1 per person.
    Mostima attacks enemies near the owner's cursor. Gain 1 SP per second.
    Right click with 63 SP to activate Key of Chronology, enhancing Mostima's attacks.
    Passively reduces all enemies movement speed.

  • Lancet-2

    Left click to summon Lancet-2. Max 1 per person.
    Lancet-2 does not attack, instead healing nearby allies.
    Has a long redeployment time.

  • Raiden Shogun

    Left click to summon Raiden Shogun's Eye of Stormy Judgment. Max 1 per person.
    Attacks from allies with the Eye of Stormy Judgment will trigger a coordinated attack.
    This coordinated attack has a 0.9s cooldown.

Stuff that aren't notable weapons

Homa Pickaxes

Homa Pickaxe buffs can be individually toggled via the in-game config.

  • R1 Homa

    Grants greatly increased item grab range and speed.
    Grants significantly increased wall and tile placement speed, and mining speed.
    Grants light emitting from the player and night vision potion effect.

  • R2 Homa

    Grants slightly increased life and mana regen, akin to the Campfire and Star in a Bottle.
    Grants Spelunker and Hunter potion effects.

  • R3 Homa

    Grants a few Cell Phone accessory effects.
    Grants 5 defense and +1 minion, akin to the Bast Statue and Bewitching Table.

  • R4 Homa

    Grants several mimic accessory effects: Cross Necklace, Panic Necklace, and Philosopher Stone.
    Grants the Frog Leg effects and the Mana Flower effect.

  • R5 Homa

    Grants the Bundle of Balloons' effects, and immunity to knockback and fall damage.

  • R6 Homa

    Grants 12% (vanilla) damage increase.
    When not moving, slightly increases life regen, defense, minion and sentry count.
